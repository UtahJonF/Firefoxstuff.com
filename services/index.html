Unsung Hero
The unsung hero of internet services is Spamgourmet.  With this gem, you create a base address, then add a prefix for each company you give it to.  For example, if your base is ender@spamgourmet.com you could add individual and specific prefixes such as cnn.ender@spamgourmet.com, classmates.ender@spamgourmet.com, microsoft.ender@spamgourmet.com, and facebook.ender@spamgourmet.com.  Then anytime you get email to one of these addresses, it forwards to your actual email address and you know who is selling your address!
