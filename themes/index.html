Firefox has a lot of great themes that make it more fun to use the internet.

    The most IE8-like theme with Vista Aero style.
  • myFireFox
    The theme most perfectly simulating Internet Explorer 8 by far.
  • RedShift V3
    The highly anticipated Shift V3 series is finally on it's way.
  • Gradient iCool (Blue)
    A modern glassy dark theme with unique animated interface. A perfect mixture of black and blue.

    A coat of Chrome for Firefox. Chromifox is a bright and soft blue theme for Firefox.
